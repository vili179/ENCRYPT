About ENCRYPT
Dual-layer privacy framework

Digital communication routinely carries highly sensitive information across channels subject to observation. Encryption protects content, yet the presence of encrypted data itself remains exposed.

ENCRYPT integrates encryption with image steganography using LSB embedding. Encrypted messages are concealed within ordinary images — encryption secures the content, steganography hides its existence. Now supports PNG, JPEG, JPG, HEIC, HEIF.

⚙️ How It Works
01
Encryption
Message is XOR-encrypted with your password → randomized binary data.
02
Steganographic Embedding
Encrypted bits replace Least Significant Bits of image pixels. Visually imperceptible.
03
Transmission
Output image looks identical to original. Can be shared freely.
04
Extraction & Decryption
Receiver extracts LSB data, reconstructs encrypted message, decrypts with correct key.
